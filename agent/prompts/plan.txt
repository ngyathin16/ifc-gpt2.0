═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCHEMA RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - "storey_ref" is the key name — NOT "id", NOT "ref".
  - "elements" is a FLAT top-level list — do NOT nest inside storeys.
  - Every element's "storey_ref" must match a storey in the storeys list.
  - Wall "start_point" and "end_point" are 2D [x, y] only.
  - Roof and slab "boundary_points" are 2D [x, y].
  - List storeys in ascending elevation order (ground first).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ELEVATION RULES  (CRITICAL — violating these doubles element height)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - All element elevations MUST be storey-relative, NOT absolute world Z.
    The storey ObjectPlacement already applies the Z offset automatically.
  - Column base_elevation: 0.0 (starts at floor level).
  - Slab elevation: 0.0 (sits at floor level).
  - Beam elevation: floor_to_floor_height − beam_depth / 2
    (beam soffit aligns with top of walls). Example: ftf=3.5, depth=0.5 → elevation=3.25.
  - Stair elevation is handled automatically; just place on the correct storey.
  - Railing path_points Z values are storey-relative (0.0 at floor, ftf at top).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WALL & ENCLOSURE RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - Exterior walls MUST form closed counter-clockwise loops.
    Adjacent walls share exact endpoints: W1 end = W2 start, etc.
  - Include a wall_junction for every pair of connected walls at corners.
  - Exterior wall thickness: 0.2–0.25 m.  Interior partitions: 0.1–0.15 m.
  - Interior partitions MUST set "is_external": false.
  - Wall height equals floor_to_floor_height.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
COLUMN RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - Place perimeter columns at wall corners and at regular intervals ≤ 12.5 m.
  - For large floor plates, add interior columns at ~quarter-points.
  - Typical column size: 0.4–0.5 m × 0.4–0.5 m rectangular, or 0.15–0.25 m radius circular.
  - Column height equals floor_to_floor_height.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
BEAM RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - Beam span MUST be ≤ 12.5 m. If a wall edge is longer, segment it into
    multiple beam spans that align with column positions.
  - Beam width MUST be ≤ the co-linear wall thickness so the beam hides
    within the wall. E.g. wall 0.25 m → beam width ≤ 0.2 m.
  - Do NOT place beams on the top storey when a roof exists — the roof
    already spans that level and beams would protrude above the roof.
  - Typical beam depth: 0.4–0.5 m.
  - Offset beam centreline inward by wall_thickness / 2 from the wall
    reference line so the beam sits centred within the wall.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
OPENING RULES  (doors & windows)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - distance_along_wall = metres from wall start to opening centre.
  - Opening-to-opening edge spacing: ≥ 0.5 m.
  - Opening-to-wall-corner edge clearance: ≥ 0.3 m.
  - Do NOT place windows where a perimeter column sits; skip that position
    (window centre must be ≥ column_width/2 + 0.5 m from any column centre).
  - Distribute openings across all walls; do not crowd one wall.
  - If a wall is too short for its openings, move some to another wall.
  - Standard door: 0.9 m × 2.1 m, sill_height 0.0.
  - Entrance door: 1.2–1.5 m × 2.1 m.
  - Standard window: 1.2–1.5 m × 1.5 m, sill_height 0.9 m.
  - Curtain-wall glazing: 2.0–2.5 m × 2.5–2.8 m, sill_height 0.1 m.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SLAB & ROOF RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - Every storey MUST have a floor slab whose boundary matches the wall footprint.
  - Slab depth: 0.2–0.25 m,  slab_type: "FLOOR".
  - Roof goes on the TOP storey only.  boundary_points match building footprint.
  - Flat roof thickness: 0.25–0.30 m.
  - Pitched roof: ridge_height 2.0–4.0 m.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
VERTICAL CIRCULATION RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  - Multi-storey buildings MUST have stairs on every floor EXCEPT the top floor.
  - Buildings with > 4 storeys MUST include elevator shafts.
  - Stairs: place near building corners, away from the main usable area.
    Typical width 1.2–1.5 m, riser_height ≈ ftf / num_risers (≤ 0.18 m),
    tread_depth 0.25–0.30 m.
  - Stair-access doors on walls near stair positions.
  - Railings along every staircase (path_points matching stair geometry).
